BMAD Method

AI-Driven Agile Development Framework

What is BMAD?

BMAD stands for Breakthrough Method of Agile AI Driven Development. It's the most comprehensive Agile AI Driven Development framework with true scale-adaptive intelligence that adjusts from bug fixes to enterprise systems.

Traditional AI tools do the thinking for you, producing average results. BMAD agents act as expert collaborators who guide you through a structured process to bring out your best thinking in partnership with the AI.

100% free and open source. No paywalls. No gated content. No gated Discord. Just pure AI-powered development goodness.

Quick Start

Get started in seconds with just one command:

$ npx bmad-method install

Follow the installer prompts, then open your AI IDE (Claude Code, Cursor, etc.) in your project folder. That's it! You're ready to BMAD.

Pro Tip: Not sure what to do next? Just run /bmad-help anytime. It tells you exactly what's next and what's optional. You can even ask /bmad-help I just finished the architecture, what do I do next?

Two Development Paths

BMAD adapts to your needs with two complementary approaches:

Quick Flow
For bug fixes, refactoring, and small features. Two commands: quick-spec and quick-dev. No ceremony, just results.
Full Method
For new products, major features, and architectural decisions. Product Brief → PRD → Architecture → Implementation.

When to Use Each

Quick Flow is perfect for:

  • Bug fixes and patches
  • Refactoring existing code
  • Small, well-understood features
  • Prototyping and spikes

Full Method is needed for:

  • New products or platforms needing stakeholder alignment
  • Major features spanning multiple components
  • Architectural decisions (database, APIs, services)
  • Unclear or contested requirements
